Number of MIPs on UNL Campus Decrease Significantly

By: Michael Stanek

This graph maps out all of the minor in posession or "M.I.P." charges given by the University of Nebraska-Lincoln Police Department from January 2014 to October 12, 2015. Though the data set from 2015 is not complete, there is a significant drop in MIP charges on campus in comparison to 2014.In the year 2014, UNLPD officers gave out 390 MIPs in total. By October 12, 2015 UNLPD officers have issued out 208 MIPs which makes for a 46% drop in MIPs on the University of Nebraska-Lincoln campus. That figure is sure to drop a bit by the end of the year, however the drop will probably still be significant by the beggining of the year 2016.

The graph below is in order from the beggining of January 2014 and ending on October 12, 2015. Each bar is labeled with the number of MIPs given out by UNLPD officers at the end of each month. These MIPs come from both drug and alcohol posession by minors on campus.
